CampusArena 🎮🏆

CampusArena is a competitive sports gaming platform designed to connect students who enjoy sports video games and want to compete with each other through organized tournaments.

The platform allows users to create profiles, join tournaments, get matched with other players, and track their progress through tournament brackets. CampusArena aims to create a fun and competitive gaming environment for students on campus.

CampusArena initially focuses on popular sports games such as EA Sports FC 24 and NBA 2K24, with plans to expand to more sports titles in the future.

Vision

CampusArena aims to become the central hub for competitive gaming among university students by providing a platform where players can easily organize tournaments, compete, track rankings, and connect with fellow gamers.

Tournament System

CampusArena allows administrators to create tournaments for sports games.

Supported tournament formats include:

Single Elimination

Double Elimination (future feature)

Round Robin (future feature)

Players can join multiple tournaments at the same time.

Example tournaments:

FC Campus Cup

Weekend Champions League

Campus NBA Challenge

Matchmaking and Fixtures

Once a tournament reaches its required number of players:

The system automatically generates match fixtures.

Players are paired randomly or based on seeding.

Matches are displayed on the tournament bracket.

Example bracket structure:

Quarter Finals
Player A vs Player B
Player C vs Player D
Player E vs Player F
Player G vs Player H

Semi Finals
Winner Match 1 vs Winner Match 2
Winner Match 3 vs Winner Match 4

Final
Winner SF1 vs Winner SF2

Match Result Submission

After playing their match:

A player submits the match score.

A screenshot of the match result is uploaded as proof.

The opponent confirms the result.

The system updates the tournament bracket automatically.

Example:

Player1 3 - 1 Player2

The winner advances to the next round.
